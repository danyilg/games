About The Game

My Summer Car is a unique combination of a car building simulator and a survival game set in the Finnish countryside during the summer of 1995.

As the player, you start with a disassembled car (the legendary Satsuma AMP) in your parents' garage. Your goal is to assemble the car from scratch, pass the inspection, and compete in the local rally.

But it's not just about cars - you'll need to manage your character's needs, work odd jobs for money, deal with Finnish bureaucracy, and survive the harsh realities of rural Finland in the 90s.

Key Features

Realistic Car Building

Assemble your Satsuma AMP from hundreds of individual parts with realistic mechanics. Every bolt and hose must be properly installed.

Survival Elements

Manage hunger, thirst, fatigue, and hygiene. Buy food, drink beer (responsibly), and sleep to keep your character alive.

Open World

Explore the Finnish countryside with its dirt roads, small towns, and lakes. Visit the pub, go fishing, or just enjoy the scenery.

Bureaucracy

Deal with Finnish bureaucracy - register your car, get insurance, and pass the inspection before you can legally drive.

Permadeath

If you die (from accidents, alcohol poisoning, etc.), your save is deleted. Play carefully or enable "permadeath off" mode.

NPC Interactions

Meet various NPCs including your parents, neighbors, mechanics, and the infamous drunk uncle. Each has their own personality.

Vehicles

Satsuma AMP (1995)

Your main project car. A fictional Japanese compact car that needs to be assembled from scratch. Based on the Datsun 100A.
